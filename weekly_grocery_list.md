## 💡 Meal Prep Tips

1. **Sunday prep**: Wash and chop veggies, cook rice/quinoa in bulk
2. **Marinate proteins**: Prep chicken and shrimp marinades night before
3. **Portion snacks**: Divide trail mix, crackers, cheese into grab-and-go bags
4. **Smoothie packs**: Pre-portion frozen fruit + spinach in bags for quick blending
5. **Hard boil eggs**: Make 6-8 for quick protein snacks
